What Makes Our Lead Generation Different

Find and Drive is consumer-focused at its core. Before a single rand is spent on advertising, we invest time in researching, understanding, and analysing South African car-buying audiences. Most campaigns in this industry are product-first — whether the focus is the vehicle or the finance offer. Our approach starts with a buyer-first view combined with deep product and industry understanding.

The result is campaigns built around real buyer profiles rather than assumptions. Accuracy comes first: understanding exactly who is in the market, how they behave, and where untapped demand exists. Efficiency follows from that. That sequence is what makes paid advertising genuinely effective — not just expensive.

Stage 01 · Commercial Problem Definition
Understand the Vehicle, the Market & the Commercial Reality
We begin with the vehicle and the underlying commercial challenge. Example: Ford Puma residual values underperformed expectations, creating pressure to improve unit movement. That becomes the strategic brief: who is most likely to buy this vehicle, and what value proposition will motivate action?
Stage 02 · Market Discovery
Analyse Consumer Sentiment & Demand Signals
We analyse search behaviour, market demand, consumer sentiment, and the vehicle’s positioning to identify audiences naturally aligned to the product. Decisions are driven by real market signals — not assumptions or broad demographic targeting.
Stage 03 · Audience Intelligence
Match the Vehicle to the Right Buyer Profile
Once the opportunity audience is identified, we build a detailed buyer profile across demographics, behavioural patterns, affordability indicators, lifestyle alignment, and intent signals. This level of profiling enables campaigns that resonate more effectively and convert with greater efficiency.
Stage 04 · Performance Execution
Reduce Wastage. Optimise for Purchase Intent.
OEMs have already invested heavily in broad brand awareness. We do not compete at the awareness layer. Instead, we focus on highly specific, in-market audiences — targeting consumers showing measurable buying intent rather than passive browsing behaviour.
Our Positioning
Audience-First.
Not Product-First.
Most campaigns in the automotive market are product-led — built around a specific vehicle, finance offer, or promotional campaign, with customer acquisition as the primary objective. Find and Drive takes a fundamentally different approach. We start with the buyer: understanding who they are, what they value, their affordability profile, and where genuine purchase intent exists. The result is stronger lead quality, higher conversion efficiency, and campaigns aligned to real market demand rather than broad reach.
